Crafting Dreams Since 2020

Born from a passion for creating beautiful jewelry, Beads Z started as a small workshop in Northeast India. Today, we're proud to be a cherished part of the creative community, providing high-quality beads and supplies to crafters worldwide.

Our mission is to inspire creativity and self-expression through our carefully curated collection of beads, charms, and DIY kits. Each product is selected with love and attention to detail, ensuring that our customers have access to the best materials for their creative journey.
